The News

This was a group project between myself, Ivonne Ortega, and Will Alford. The app pulls in news from the New York Times API, both on a periodic basis while connected to a network and charging as well as on demand from the user.

I would like to be able to pull in news articles from other news outlets. Additionally, I would like to optimize the experience for tablets, as well as allow for direct article sharing to social media within the app, so the user does not have to open other apps.
